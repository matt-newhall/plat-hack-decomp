# Mechanic Changes

Here is the list of bonus/later generation mechanics implemented in Pokemon Tempered Platinum, on top of the base generation IV games.

## Tempered Platinum Changes

- Mechanics match up to gen 9 (with some tweaks, see here and below).
- Switch AI and Move AI have been significantly revamped.
- Critical hit rate is still 1/16, but critical hits deal 1.5x damage, not 2x.
  - Consequently, Pokemon with the ability Sniper deal 2.25x damage when they land critical hits, not 3x.
- Drizzle/Drought/Sand Stream/Snow Warning are no longer limited to 5 turns.
- Explosion & Selfdestruct still halve the target's Defense.
- Switcheroo/Trick swap items permanently.
- Knock Off knocks items off permanently, in both wild and trainer battles.
- Attract disregards gender, and works on genderless Pokemon.
- Added Magma Storm, Trick Room, enemy Tailwind and Sun Overworld Weather Conditions.
- Destiny Bond will not fail if successfully executed on the previous turn.
- 3 abilities are now unavailable: Frisk, Simple, Pickup.
  - Frisk was too annoying to implement.
  - Simple was too annoying to implement.
  - Pickup has been removed for being too overpowered for a Nuzlocke with the latest similar item tables.
- The following abilities' overworld effects act the same way they did as of generation VIII:
  - Intimidate and Keen Eye still decrease random wild encounter rate by 50%.
  - Sand Veil and Snow Cloak still decrease random wild encounter rate when in overworld Sandstorm and Hail weather respectively by 50%.
  - Synchronize will still always change static encounter's natures. It will also not change roaming Pokemon's natures.

## Mechanics from future generations

- All moves and abilities have been updated to perform how they would function in their latest appearance in the series.
- Grass-types are immune to powder and spore-type moves/abilities.
- Ghost-types can not be trapped.
- Electric-types are immune to paralysis.
- Ice-types gain 1.5x Defence in Hail.
- Burn only inflicts 1/16 damage per turn, down from 1/8.
  - Consequently, Pokemon with the ability Heatproof take 1/32 damage per turn instead of 1/16.
- Confused Pokemon have a 1/3 chance to damage themselves in confusion, down from 1/2.
- Paralysis cuts speed by 1/2, not 1/4.
- The steel type has lost its resistances to both Dark and Ghost-type moves.
- Sound-based moves can hit through Substitute.
- Hidden Power always has 60BP.

## Generation IV Bugs Status

Pokemon Platinum and generation IV games in general have a number of bugs present in the base version of the game. Some of these have been fixed, but I'll give a run down here

### Fixed Bugs

**Fire Fang Wonder Guard glitch** ✅

Fire Fang and Beat Up both do not bypass Wonder Guard.

**Rage glitch** ✅

using Rage and then another move now removes the Rage volatile status condition, instead of removing all other volatile status conditions.
