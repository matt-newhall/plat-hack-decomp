# Implemented changes

Herein are all of overworld ability changes that have been made to moves as part of the base engine for Pokemon Tempered Platinum.

## Changes

Cute Charm ✅
- Chance to manipulate gender of wild encounters changed from 66.7% => 66%

Flash Fire, Lightning Rod, Storm Drain ✅
- 50% chance of encountering a Fire, Electric and Water-type respectively.

Hustle, Pressure, Vital Spirit ✅(UNTESTED)
- Removes 50% chance that a Pokemon will be forced to the upper bound of their encounter level range

Immunity ✅(Needs to be out of decomp)
- Now does nothing, as no poison outside of battles

Pickup ✅
- Updated table to ORAS Pickup.

Quick Feet, Stench, White Smoke ✅
- Decreases the chance of hidden encounters by 87.5%

Sticky Hold, Suction Cups ✅
- Bug removed

Super Luck ✅
- Shares the effect of Compoundeyes

Synchronize ✅
- 100% chance to change the nature of wild encounters to the nature of this Pokemon (up from 50%)
